Welcome

Thank you very much for participating in this experiment. During the experiment you are not allowed to communicate with other participants. Please only use the programs and functions that were designed for the experiment. If you have a question, please send a message in the Zoom chat. We will answer your question privately. Do not post questions in the Zoom chat. If the question is relevant for all participants, we will notify the other participants. If you break these rules, we may have to exclude you from the experiment and from payment.

Urn Draws

In this experiment we will secretly draw two balls for you and then put them back in the urn. The first ball is drawn from Urn 1, which contains {{ high_ability_balls }} blue and {{ low_ability_balls }} red balls. This means that the probability of drawing a blue ball is {{ high_ability_prob }}%. The second ball is drawn from Urn 2, which contains {{ privileged_balls }} white and {{ nonprivileged_balls }} black balls. This means that the probability of drawing a white ball is {{ privileged_prob }}%.

Urn 1 Urn 2

You will interact with another participant in this experiment. We will refer to this person as your partner. We will also secretly draw two balls for your partner using the same procedure. We will not tell you which ball we drew for you or your partner until the end of the experiment.

Decision 1

You will have to choose between three doors. There is a prize of {{ Constants.choice_payoff }} behind some of the doors. If you pick a winning door, you will receive the prize. The number of winning doors depends on the ball, which we drew for you from Urn 1.

  • If the ball drawn from Urn 1 is blue, {{ Constants.number_of_correct_choices_high }} of the doors are winning doors.
  • If the ball drawn from Urn 1 is red, {{ Constants.number_of_correct_choices_low }} of the doors are winning doors.
We will not tell you whether you or your partner chose a winning door until the end of the experiment.

Information and Guesses

Afterwards, you will receive some information. We will show you a card that can be either blue or red. If we drew a blue ball for you, we will draw a card from a pile that contains {{ pile_correct_cards }} blue and {{ pile_incorrect_cards }} red cards. If we drew a red ball for you, we will draw a card from a pile that contains {{ pile_incorrect_cards }} blue and {{ pile_correct_cards }} red cards. The probability that your card's color matches your ball's color is {{ pile_correct_prob }}%. Your partner will also see a card that depends on their ball's color. You will never learn about the color of your partner's card and your partner will never learn the color of your card.

Furthermore, we will tell you if you receive a yellow star or not. You will only receive a yellow star if we drew a white ball for you from Urn 2 and if you chose a winning door in the previous choice. If either the ball is black or you did not choose a winning door (or both), you will not receive a yellow star. Your partner may also receive a yellow star according to the same rules. {{ if public }} We will let you know whether your partner received a yellow star, and your partner will know whether you received one. {{ else }} We will not tell you whether your partner received a yellow star until the end of the experiment, and your partner will not know whether you received one. {{ endif }}

After you have received the information we will ask you to evaluate how likely some statements are. For each statement there is a prize of {{ Constants.beliefs_payoff }}. The probability of winning the prize depends on your guess and whether the statement is true. To maximize the probability of earning the prize you should simply guess how likely each statement is true on average. We will show you the exact probabilities when you make the choice.

Survey

After the experiment there will be a short survey, for which you will earn some extra cash.

Payments

You will be paid either for the door choices in Decision 1 and Decision 2, or for your guesses, but not for both. Both are equally likely. At the end of the experiment, we will inform you whether you are paid for the doors or for your guesses. After the experiment, we will transfer your earning via Paypal to the email address that you specified in the beginning of the experiment.
